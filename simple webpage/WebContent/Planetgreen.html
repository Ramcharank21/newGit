Atmosphere

Atmosphere consists of various gases like oxygen,nitrogen,carbon dioxide etc..
Earths Atmosphere

The atmosphere protects life on earth by shielding it from incoming ultraviolet (UV) radiation,
keeping the planet warm through insulation, and preventing extremes between day and night temperatures.
The sun heats layers of the atmosphere causing it to convect driving air movement and weather patterns around the world.
